Project Overview

This project is a conceptual digital wallet app designed to understand and solve common usability issues users face while making everyday payments. The main intention of this case study was not to build a live product, but to show my UX thinking, research approach, and problem-solving skills.
The app focuses on simple payments, clear transaction feedback, and user trust, which are often missing in many existing wallet applications.

Platform: Android & iOS

My Role: Lead UI/UX Designer

Duration: 3 Weeks

Tools Used: Figma, FigJam, Google Forms
